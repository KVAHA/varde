The Platform

About Nordic Mining Intelligence

An independent analytical platform focused on Nordic mining companies, mining jurisdictions, and the strategic forces shaping the sector.

What
What We Publish

Investment-grade research on Nordic mining companies, Norwegian and Swedish mining jurisdictions, and the geopolitical context shaping critical minerals supply chains in Northern Europe. Reports cover valuation, permitting, project quality, risks, and execution.

How
Analytical Standards

Research is built on primary sources: public filings, permit applications, regulatory documents, geological surveys, financial statements, and direct engagement with the operational realities of Nordic resource extraction. No promotional relationships.

Why
Why This Platform

Nordic mining occupies a strategically significant position in European critical minerals supply chains. Serious, independent analysis of this sector — covering both investment and strategic dimensions — is underserved. This platform is built to fill that gap.

Research Principles

Nordic Mining Intelligence applies investment-grade analytical standards to a sector that often receives either promotional coverage or no coverage at all. The research is independent, evidence-based, and focused on what actually matters for investors, operators, and those assessing where to allocate capital or establish operations in Nordic mining.

  • Independent — no promotional relationships
  • Primary-source driven
  • Focused on value, risk, and execution
  • Covers listed companies and strategic projects
  • Deep coverage of Norway and Sweden
  • Includes permitting, financing, and geopolitical risk
  • Open-access research platform
  • Investment and operational perspectives
