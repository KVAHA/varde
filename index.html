The Research Approach

Nordic Mining Intelligence publishes independent, evidence-based analysis of Nordic mining companies, mining jurisdictions, and the strategic forces shaping the sector. The research is built on primary sources, public filings, regulatory documents, and direct engagement with the operational realities of Nordic resource extraction.

  • Independent — no promotional relationships
  • Primary-source driven — filings, permits, data
  • Focused on value, risk, and project economics
  • Covers Norway and Sweden in depth
  • Investment-grade analytical standards
  • Includes permitting, financing, and execution risk
